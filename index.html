About Me

Liang Xie received his Ph.D. from the School of Electronic and Computer Engineering at Peking University. He is currently an Assistant Professor in the School of Computer Science and Technology at Guangdong University of Technology. His research focuses on 3D data compression and reconstruction, point cloud and 3D Gaussian compression, perceptual distortion modeling, multimodal foundation models, and embodied AI.

He has published more than 20 papers in journals and conferences including IEEE TPAMI, IEEE TBC, IEEE TIM, APSIPA Transactions, AAAI, ACM MM, ICRA, and DCC. He has also submitted more than 25 AVS/IEEE DCSC standardization proposals, with over 10 adopted, and led open-source point cloud compression libraries such as LearningPCC and PCHMVision.
