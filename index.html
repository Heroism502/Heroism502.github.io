    👨‍🎓 About Me

    I am currently an Assistant Professor at the School of Computer Science and Technology, Guangdong University of Technology. I received my Ph.D. from the School of Electronic and Computer Engineering, Peking University. My research focuses on 3D data compression and reconstruction, with particular emphasis on point cloud compression, 3D Gaussian representation and coding, perceptual quality modeling, multimodal foundation models, and embodied intelligence.

    I have published over 20 papers in leading journals and conferences, including IEEE TPAMI, IEEE TBC, IEEE TIM, APSIPA Transactions, AAAI, ACM MM, ICRA, and DCC. I have also submitted more than 25 AVS/IEEE DCSC standardization proposals as the first proposer, among which over 10 have been adopted, and several others are under exploration experiments. In addition, I have filed 15 invention patents related to visual media coding, point cloud compression, multimodal data processing, and 3D reconstruction.

    I actively contribute to research software, datasets, and academic service. I led the development of open-source point cloud compression libraries including LearningPCC and PCHMVision, and constructed large-scale datasets such as PKU-DPCC for dynamic point cloud compression, PKU-JND for just noticeable difference modeling of point cloud compression, and PKU-GS for UAV-based Gaussian point cloud data. I also serve as a reviewer for leading journals and conferences in multimedia and computer vision, including TPAMI, TIP, TMM, TCE, AAAI, ACM MM, and ICRA.
